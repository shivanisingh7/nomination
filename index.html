Young Alumni Awards

Thank you for taking the time to help us celebrate the incredible achievements of our alumni. The Young Alumni Awards recognize the outstanding accomplishments and achievements of IIIT-Delhi alumni who are making a difference in the world through their professional and/or academic achievements, or through their significant contributions to the community.

The nominations for the Young Alumni Awards will be welcomed from anyone (Faculty, Staff, Students, Alumni) familiar with the work, character, and/or achievements of IIITD alumnus/alumna. To nominate your candidate, please fill out the nomination form latest by 31st May.

Nomination Criteria:
The nominee should be a graduate of IIIT-Delhi.
The nominee should have a significant record of professional accomplishment and/or public service.
The nominee should be a graduate of not more than ten years from the institute and should be under 40 years of age.
